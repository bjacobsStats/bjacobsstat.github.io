About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Statistics at Iowa State University. I have previously earned my BS and MS in Statistics from the University of Iowa. I then worked for a few years as a Biostatistician at the Center for International Blood and Marrow Transplant Research (CIBMTR) at the Medical College of Wisconsin.

My research interests include Bayesian statistics, probability, and statistical computing and graphics.
